Instructions
Getting Started ▸
Open the FS Fire panel from the toolbar. Select the Fires tab to load live VIIRS satellite hotspots, or choose a mission from the Missions tab. Head to the Drop tab to configure your aircraft's retardant system.
Drop System ▸
Use Drop Now for manual drops, or arm GPS Drop to auto-release over a map-selected target. Physics account for altitude, ground speed, and heading. For helicopters, enable the Bucket mode and scoop water from any lake or river below 25 ft AGL at under 10 kts.
Multiplayer ▸
Host a session from the MP tab and share your 8-digit code or QR link. All fires, prop objects, and drop VFX sync in real time across VATSIM, IVAO, and native MSFS multiplayer. Use session chat to coordinate with your crew.
Missions ▸
Browse 325+ missions on the global map. Each mission tracks your drop efficiency — minimum drops divided by actual drops. Gold requires 80%+, Silver 67%+. Daily Challenges double your score. Missions can be hosted in multiplayer so the whole crew earns credit.
