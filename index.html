// Core Features

EVERYTHING YOU NEED
TO FIGHT FIRE

FS Fire brings the intensity of aerial firefighting into MSFS with realistic simulation, live satellite data, and deep multiplayer support.

🔥

Live Wildfire Hotspots

FS Fire pulls real active wildfire data from the VIIRS NOAA-20 NRT satellite at 375m resolution, refreshed every 30 minutes by a cloud backend. Each hotspot's fire radiative power (FRP) is mapped directly to a sim object size — so a major California blaze spawns differently than a small brush fire.

🛩️

Physics-Based Drop System

Drop calculations factor your altitude AGL, ground speed, and heading to place retardant or water exactly where physics says it should land. Elliptical splash zones grow with altitude and airspeed, forward travel is modeled, and hits are computed against a dynamic fire radius. Works with any aircraft via the FS Fire VFX engine, or with native MSFS firefighting capable aircraft. Choose from unlimited drops, or enable the "Tank Refill" capability.

🌐

Full Multiplayer Support

Host a session and fly with a crew across VATSIM, IVAO, or native MSFS multiplayer. Fire state, prop objects, VFX drops, and retardant hits all sync in real time. Guests receive live fire updates within a 200 nm radius of the host, and drop VFX trigger correctly on any MP aircraft within 1 nm — even aircraft you can't directly observe.

🪣

Helicopter Bucket

Spawn a suspended helicopter water bucket under any helicopter, complete with water scooping capability over any body of water and realistic drop physics and effects.

🎯

Mission System

325+ handcrafted missions at real-world POIs globally — wildfires, vehicle incidents, boat emergencies, and structure fires. Each mission logs your drop efficiency (minimum drops ÷ actual drops × 100%) and awards Gold, Silver, or Bronze medals. Daily Challenges double your points, Weekly Goals track ongoing progress, and a persistent logbook stores your best result per mission. Missions can be completed solo or in multiplayer with the help of others.

🗺️

Live Moving Map

A multi-layer map follows your aircraft in real time using a 30 fps physics interpolation loop. Toggle track-up or north-up, use the declutter control to filter between all markers, fires only, or none. Click any fire marker for a popup with size and AGL data, click the map to get coordinates for a precision GPS drop, and place or drag simobject props directly on the map. In multiplayer, other players in your session appear on the map with live position updates.

🧱

Simobject Scene Builder

Browse the MSFS simobject catalog — hundreds of default objects, direct user entry, or MSFS 2024 file system scanned simobjects — spanning aircraft, ground vehicles, boats, animals, humans, and landmarks. Spawn any object by map-click or coordinate, rotate its heading, and drag it to reposition. Save and load entire scenes including fire layouts, with optional prop-only or fires-only saves.

💬

Session Chat & Coordination

Built-in session chat syncs messages across all connected players via HMAC-authenticated cloud relay. Share your session with an 8-digit code or a QR code linking to the live session browser. Host can transfer control mid-session, and the admin panel tracks GamerTag history and session analytics.

🌍

Six Languages

Full UI localization in English, Italian, German, French, Spanish, and Portuguese across every panel, button, and tooltip.

// Getting Started

READY IN MINUTES

Get from installation to fighting your first wildfire in just a few steps.

01

Download from Marketplace

Find FS Fire on the Microsoft Flight Simulator in-game Marketplace and install it directly.

02

Open the Panel

In any flight, open the FS Fire panel from the toolbar. Live hotspots load automatically from satellite data.

03

Pick a Fire or Mission

Select a live wildfire or choose from 325+ missions. The fire spawns near your current location.

04

Fly & Fight

Fly your tanker, helicopter, or scooper to the fire and start dropping. Invite friends to join your session.
